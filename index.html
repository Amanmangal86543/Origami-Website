Interesting facts about Camels:

  1. Camel's ears are furry.
  2. Camels are able to walk on sand because of their specially designed feet.
  3. When they find water, they try to drink as much as possible.

Interesting facts about Pandas:

  1. Pandas are really good climbers and swimmers.
  2. Pandas go from pink to white and black or brown.
  3. Adult Pandas can eat upto 12-38 KGs of Bamboo per day.

Interesting facts about Pigeons:

  1. Pigeons have been present with humans from thousands of years.
  2. They are one of only a small number of species to pass the 'mirror test' – a test of self recognition.
  3. They can also recognise each letter of the human alphabet and differentiate between photographs.

Interesting facts about Teddy Bears:

  1. The Teddy Bear was named after President Theodore Roosevelt.
  2. German toy company Steiff created 500 teddy bears to honor the victims of the titanic,The “mourning bears”
  3. Magellan T Bear boarded Space Shuttle Discovery in 1995 as part of a school project.

Interesting facts about Chameleons:

  1. Unlike many lizards, Chameleons can't regrow their tails.
  2. Their eyes can swivel around in two different directions simultaneously.
  3. Chameleon spit is unbelievably sticky.

Interesting facts about Cicada:

  1. Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans.
  2. Their lengthy life cycles may help them evade predators.
  3. Their wings repel water and bacteria.
